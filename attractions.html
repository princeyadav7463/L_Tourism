Top Tourist Attractions in Latehar

  • Netarhat – Known as the Queen of Chotanagpur, famous for sunrise/sunset points and pine forests.
  • Lodh Falls – The highest waterfall in Jharkhand, surrounded by dense forest.
  • Betla National Park – A wildlife sanctuary with elephants, tigers, and Palamu Fort ruins.
  • Koel River – Offers scenic beauty and calm surroundings for nature lovers.
  • Sugga Waterfalls – Hidden gem perfect for trekking and photography.
  • Palamu Fort – Historical ruins within the Betla forest, a must-visit for history lovers.
  • Tattaha Falls – A tranquil location away from the crowd, ideal for peace seekers.
